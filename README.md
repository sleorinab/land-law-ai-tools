\# Land Law NZ — AI Prompt Tools



AI prompt templates built for a New Zealand property law firm.

Used in production to assist with document review, client

communications, and workflow automation.



\## Prompts Included



\### 1. Property Title Review



\*\*Problem it solves:\*\* Reviewing a record of title manually 

requires a lawyer or support staff to read through legal 

descriptions, check for encumbrances, easements, covenants, 

and caveats, then summarise findings for the client. This 

prompt automates the initial review layer, flagging items 

that need attention before a lawyer does the final check.



\*\*Tools used:\*\* Claude API



\*\*Time saved:\*\* Estimated 20–30 minutes per title review. 

At 5–10 reviews per week, that is 2–5 hours of staff time 

recovered weekly.



\*\*Example output:\*\* Structured summary listing registered 

owner, legal description, any encumbrances found, easements 

noted, caveats present, and a plain-English risk flag 

section for client communication.



\---



\### 2. Client Email Draft



\*\*Problem it solves:\*\* Property transactions involve 

repetitive client communication at predictable stages — 

offer acceptance, conditions satisfaction, pre-settlement, 

and post-settlement. Drafting these emails from scratch 

each time is time-consuming and inconsistent across staff. 

This prompt generates a professional, NZ-contextualised 

draft from minimal inputs (client name, property address, 

transaction stage) that staff review and send in under 

2 minutes.



\*\*Tools used:\*\* Claude API



\*\*Time saved:\*\* Estimated 10–15 minutes per email. 

At 15–20 client emails per week, that is approximately 

3–4 hours of staff time saved weekly.



\*\*Example output:\*\* Professional email draft with correct 

NZ legal terminology, personalised greeting, clear 

transaction stage update, next steps for the client, 

and a call to action — ready to copy into ActionStep 

or send directly.
